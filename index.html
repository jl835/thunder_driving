Money Help

Getting a new vehicle is expensive, and the same goes for EV's; however, the government wants to help. If you're planning on buying a new EV, or previously purchased one in the current year, you can be eligible for up to $7,500 in credit. One main catch is that the rebate is for specific models, and it includes either fuel cell EV's or plug-in EV's. There are a couple of incentives availabile, you can find some here. One incentive program for residents in California is the Clean Vehicle Rebate Project, however it is not accepting new applicants as of November 8 2023.
